About

Angle Basin is the ambient techno project of producer Aaron Steven White, recording at Sink State in Rochester, NY.

The project is founded on dissonant harmonies, cavernous spaces, and dense angular grooves.

The debut album Eigengrau is out 7 Aug 2026.
